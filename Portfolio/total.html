  • TOTAL E&P - Qatar

    Department : Engineering Department - WareHouse
    Position Held : Materials Coordinator
    Indicative Date : July 11, 2019 – October 16, 2019 (3 months)

    Duties and Responsibilities:
    • Under the direction of the Logistic Head at GWC Main Warehouse.
      1. Served as support team on the following person as per delegated by the Logistic Base head.
      2. Material Controller – Picking team
      3. Material Controller – Receiving team
      4. Material Coordinator – Loadout’s preparation and material issuance to contractor

    • One of the main duties given by the Logistic Base Head was the following:
      1. Assigned to have an inventory of Bulk items and cables currently at Pipe yard. Making sure the Salsa code and quantity are correct.
      2. Inventory of Construction materials such as pipes, fittings, flanges, instrument and electrical items, gaskets, and consumables at the M-Rack location central warehouse. Making sure the Salsa code and quantity are correct.
      3. Inventory of Production and maintenance items such as spare parts, installation materials, valves, instrument fittings, etc. at the Tornado storages bin location.

    • During my training I got to learned about the process of SAP from the material controller. This is now I believed that the range of SAP Warehouse Management system (WMS) provides flexible, automated support in processing all goods movements and in managing stocks in warehouse complex. The system supports scheduled and efficient processing of all logistics processes within the warehouse.

    • During also my training I do understand how important the code allocation. Salsa # defined as a code on each material. The important thing is the Salsa code is to be properly labelled in each item during the receiving stages, as well up to the next label of storage bin location to issuance.

    • As an assistance to Receiving team, as an instruction from Logistic head to assist the materials controller in inspections of goods. By doing so, the Purchase order should be check against the Delivery note. Making sure the delivered items is as per Purchase order description, model number, or product code. Making sure also that the Salsa code is to be written on every item as per PO.

    • As an assistance to Picking team, the main tasks are making sure that material to be pick-up for issuance to contractor or to be send to offshore was as per Salsa code. Making sure also the quantity to be prepared and delivered was accurate. If there is any doubt on materials, the material controller is to be notified to make a judgement on the materials. Also verify the actual quantity on hand against the quantity on SAP.

    • As an assistance to material coordinator. The main work is to assist on the preparation of materials loadouts. Making sure that the delivery notes or material request is to be provided for Cargo Manifest preparation. Assist also to check the CCU wherein all materials are loaded for distribution in different platform at offshore.

    • Learned the value of Safety, Safety to you, Safety to others and Safety to All. Be vigilant in Safety procedures, hazards, and anomalies. Adhere and follow to all safety requirements by using a mandatory PPE.
